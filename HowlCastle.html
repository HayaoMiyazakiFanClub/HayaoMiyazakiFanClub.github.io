"Howl's Moving Castle" is a 2004 Japanese animated fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It tells the story of Sophie, a young woman who is cursed and transformed into an old woman.

The film explores themes of identity, acceptance, the consequences of war, and the value of compassion. It is known for its imaginative world-building, complex characters, and strong anti-war messages.
